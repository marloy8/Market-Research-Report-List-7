<p><p>The Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripter (ESWL) market is segmented into three primary types based on the technology used to generate shock waves for fragmenting kidney stones. </p><p>1. **Electrohydraulic** lithotripters utilize electrical discharges in water to produce shock waves. </p><p>2. **Electromagnetic** systems employ magnetic fields to create focused shock waves, offering efficient stone fragmentation. </p><p>3. **Piezoelectric** devices use ceramic crystals that expand and contract when electric currents flow, generating precise shock waves for targeted treatment. Each type has distinct advantages in clinical settings.</p></p>
