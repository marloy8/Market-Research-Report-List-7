<p><p>The medical metal tubing market is primarily categorized into three types: stainless steel, titanium, and nitinol (NiTi). Stainless steel is widely used for its strength and corrosion resistance, making it ideal for various medical devices. Titanium offers exceptional biocompatibility and strength-to-weight ratio, suitable for implants and surgical instruments. Nitinol, a nickel-titanium alloy, is known for its unique shape memory and superelastic properties, making it valuable in applications like stents and guidewires. Each material serves specific medical needs, enhancing device performance and patient safety.</p></p>
