<p><ul><li>Sand Water Knife</li><li>Pure Water Water Knife</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water cutting head market is divided into two primary types: sand water knives and pure water knives. Sand water knives utilize a mixture of high-pressure water and abrasive materials, such as sand, to effectively cut through hard surfaces, making them suitable for industrial applications like metal and stone cutting. Pure water knives, on the other hand, use only high-pressure water to achieve precision cutting in softer materials, such as food or textiles, providing a cleaner and more environmentally friendly option.</p></p>
