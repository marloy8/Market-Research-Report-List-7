<p><p>The Household Water Purification System market is categorized into Point of Use (POU) and Point of Entry (POE) applications. POU systems are installed at specific water outlets, providing purified water for drinking and cooking, often utilizing technologies like filtration or reverse osmosis. In contrast, POE systems treat water at the entry point of a home, ensuring all taps receive purified water, addressing broader concerns like contaminants and hardness. Both solutions enhance water quality, promoting health and convenience for households.</p></p>
