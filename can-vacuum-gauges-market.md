<p><p>The vacuum gauges market features two main types: digital and dial gauges. Digital vacuum gauges provide precise readings on electronic displays, often offering advanced features like data logging and connectivity. They are favored for their accuracy and ease of use in various applications. Dial vacuum gauges, on the other hand, utilize mechanical systems with a needle and scale to indicate pressure levels. They are more traditional, reliable, and typically require no power, making them suitable for environments where electricity is unavailable.</p></p>
