<p><strong>The Clinical Laboratory Automation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Analysis System</li><li>Under Analysis System</li><li>Post-Analysis System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clinical laboratory automation systems streamline laboratory processes across three main types: pre-analysis, under analysis, and post-analysis systems. Pre-analysis systems focus on sample management, including specimen registration and sorting. Under analysis systems encompass the analytical phase, facilitating the actual testing and measurement of samples using various instruments. Post-analysis systems manage data interpretation and reporting, ensuring accurate results are communicated efficiently. Together, these systems enhance productivity, reduce errors, and improve overall workflow in clinical laboratories.</p></p>
