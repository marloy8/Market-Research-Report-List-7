<p><p>The fresh yeast market can be categorized into two main types based on sugar content: high sugar type and low sugar type. High sugar type fresh yeast is typically used in products with elevated sugar levels, such as sweet breads and pastries, as it exhibits strong fermentation activity despite high sugar concentrations. In contrast, low sugar type fresh yeast is suited for products with lower sugar content, offering a balanced fermentation process ideal for items like regular breads and rolls. Each type plays a crucial role in achieving desired texture and flavor in baked goods.</p></p>
