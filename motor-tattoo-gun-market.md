<p><ul><li>Rotary</li><li>Coiled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor tattoo gun market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and coiled machines. Rotary tattoo guns utilize a consistent motor-driven mechanism that provides smooth, quiet operation and adjustable needle depth, making them ideal for various techniques and solid colors. In contrast, coiled machines operate with electromagnetic coils, producing a more traditional, powerful punch that excels in lining and shading but can be noisy and require more maintenance. Each type caters to different artist preferences and tattooing styles.</p></p>
