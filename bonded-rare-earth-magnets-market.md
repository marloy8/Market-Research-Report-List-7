<p><p>The bonded rare earth magnets market includes two primary types: injection molded magnets and compression bonded magnets. Injection molded magnets are produced by mixing magnetic powders with thermoplastics, allowing for complex shapes and precise tolerances, ideal for high-volume applications. Compression bonded magnets, on the other hand, involve compacting magnetic powders with a binding agent under pressure, resulting in strong yet less dense magnets suitable for specific applications that require higher magnetic performance. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing technological advances.</p></p>
