<p><p>Non-destructive testing (NDT) equipment encompasses various techniques used to evaluate materials without causing damage. X-ray testing uses radiation to reveal internal flaws, while ultrasonic testing employs high-frequency sound waves for depth analysis. Magnetic particle testing detects surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials, and penetrant flaw detection reveals surface-breaking flaws using dyes. Eddy current testing analyzes conductive materials by inducing electric currents to identify defects. Other methods include visual inspection and thermal imaging, each tailored for specific applications in quality assurance.</p></p>
