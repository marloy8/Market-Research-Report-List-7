<p><p>The disposable glove market comprises several types, primarily latex, vinyl, nitrile, and others. Latex gloves offer excellent flexibility and tactile sensitivity, making them popular in medical settings but can cause allergies. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk tasks, although less durable than latex. Nitrile gloves are resistant to chemicals and punctures, making them ideal for various applications, including medical and industrial uses.
