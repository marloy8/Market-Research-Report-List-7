Potash-mobilizing fertilizers increase potassium levels, vital for various physiological processes in plants. Other bio-based options may involve organic composts, seaweed extracts, and microbial inoculants, all contributing to sustainable agriculture by enhancing soil health, improving nutrient uptake, and fostering environmental stewardship.</p></p>
