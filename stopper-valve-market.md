<p><p>The stopper valve market encompasses various types, including globe valves and needle valves. Globe valves are designed for regulating flow and pressure, featuring a spherical body that allows for precise throttling. In contrast, needle valves provide even finer control over flow rates due to their long, tapered stem and small orifice, making them ideal for applications requiring delicate adjustments. Both valve types are crucial in industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, ensuring efficient flow management.</p></p>
