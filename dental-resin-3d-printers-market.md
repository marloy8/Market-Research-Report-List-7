<p><p>The dental resin 3D printer market comprises several key technologies. Vat Photopolymerization utilizes a liquid resin cured by UV light, offering high precision for dental applications. Fused Deposition Modeling involves extruding thermoplastic materials in layers, providing versatility but less detail. PolyJet Technology jets photopolymer droplets and cures them, achieving smooth surfaces and complex geometries. Selective Laser Sintering employs lasers to fuse powdered materials, creating durable parts. Other methods may include binder jetting and digital light processing, broadening the market's scope.</p></p>
