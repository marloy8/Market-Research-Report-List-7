<p><strong>The Emergency Beacon Transmitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Personal Locator Beacons</li><li>Emergency Position-Indicating Radio Beacons</li><li>Emergency Locator Transmitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Emergency Beacon Transmitter market consists of three main types. Personal Locator Beacons (PLBs) are portable devices that allow individuals to signal for help in emergencies, usually via satellite. Emergency Position-Indicating Radio Beacons (EPIRBs) are designed for maritime use, automatically activating in water to transmit distress signals. Emergency Locator Transmitters (ELTs) are installed in aircraft to alert search and rescue teams in case of a crash, providing precise location data to facilitate quick response efforts.</p></p>
