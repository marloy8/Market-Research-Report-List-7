<p><strong>The Seatbelt Pretensioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lap Pretensioners</li><li>Buckle Pretensioners</li><li>Retractor Pretensioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seatbelt pretensioners market comprises three main types: lap pretensioners, buckle pretensioners, and retractor pretensioners. Lap pretensioners tighten the seatbelt across the lap during a collision, enhancing safety. Buckle pretensioners work by pulling the buckle closer to the occupant, minimizing slack. Retractor pretensioners engage the seatbelt retractor to remove excess webbing, ensuring the seatbelt fits snugly. Each type plays a crucial role in improving occupant protection and reducing injury risks in the event of an accident.</p></p>
