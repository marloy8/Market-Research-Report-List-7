<p><p>The seed dressing agent market comprises two main types: chemical dressing agents and biological dressing agents. Chemical dressing agents typically include synthetic fungicides, insecticides, and growth regulators that protect seeds from pests and diseases while promoting germination. In contrast, biological dressing agents use natural organisms or substances, like beneficial microbes and plant extracts, to enhance seed vigor and resilience without harmful chemicals. Both types aim to improve crop yields and ensure healthier plants, catering to varying agricultural practices and sustainability goals.</p></p>
