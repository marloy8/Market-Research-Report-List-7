<p><ul><li>Magnetic Adjustment</li><li>Gravity Adjustment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmable valves market is primarily categorized into two types: magnetic adjustment and gravity adjustment. Magnetic adjustment valves utilize electromagnetic forces to precisely control fluid flow, allowing for rapid and accurate responses to changing conditions. In contrast, gravity adjustment valves rely on the natural force of gravity to regulate flow, providing a simpler, often cost-effective solution for various applications. Both types offer distinct advantages, making them suitable for different industries like agriculture, manufacturing, and fluid management systems.</p></p>
