<p><ul><li>Oil ImmersedTransformer</li><li>Dry-type Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic step-up transformer market comprises two primary types: oil-immersed transformers and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers are surrounded by insulating oil, which enhances heat dissipation and protects against environmental factors, making them suitable for large-scale solar installations. In contrast, dry-type transformers use air for cooling and are ideal for smaller applications or areas with strict environmental regulations, offering safety and reduced maintenance risks. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing energy conversion in photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
