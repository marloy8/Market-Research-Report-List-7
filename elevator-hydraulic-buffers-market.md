<p><p>The elevator hydraulic buffers market is categorized into two main types: tap and vertical. In contrast, vertical buffers are designed for specific applications, offering varied heights for greater cushioning and energy absorption. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing elevator safety and improving ride quality by effectively managing the forces generated during operation.</p></p>
