<p><p>Gas Cluster Ion Beams (GCIB) utilize clusters of gas atoms to create high-energy ions for surface analysis and modification. The market is divided into two main types: Sputter Beams and Analytical Beams. Sputter Beams are primarily used for material removal and thin-film deposition applications, enabling precise layer control. In contrast, Analytical Beams focus on surface characterization, facilitating detailed analysis of material composition and structure. Both types are crucial in industries like semiconductor manufacturing, materials science, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
