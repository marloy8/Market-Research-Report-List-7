<p><ul><li>Gas Ionization Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear radiation scanner market comprises three main types of detectors. Gas ionization detectors use gas-filled chambers to measure ionization caused by radiation, offering real-time measurements. Semiconductor detectors utilize solid materials to detect radiation, providing high resolution and compact size, making them suitable for various applications. Scintillation detectors employ scintillating materials that emit light when exposed to radiation, allowing for quick and sensitive detection. Each type serves critical roles in fields such as security, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
