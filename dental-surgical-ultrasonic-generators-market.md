<p><p>The Dental Surgical Ultrasonic Generators market is divided into two main types: self-excited and external excited power sources. Self-excited power sources generate ultrasonic frequencies internally, providing integrated operations with enhanced efficiency and reliability. In contrast, external excited power sources rely on external circuits or sources to create ultrasonic frequencies, allowing for more customization and flexibility in applications. Both types serve critical roles in dental surgeries, optimizing precision, reducing thermal damage, and improving overall surgical outcomes.</p></p>
