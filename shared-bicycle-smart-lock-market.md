<p><p>The Shared Bicycle Smart Lock market includes three primary unlocking types: Bluetooth Unlock, GPRS Unlock, and NB-IoT Unlocking. Bluetooth Unlock utilizes short-range wireless technology, allowing users to unlock bikes via smartphones. GPRS Unlock leverages cellular networks for real-time tracking and management, enabling remote access and monitoring. NB-IoT Unlocking employs narrowband IoT technology for enhanced connectivity, ensuring low power consumption and wider coverage, making it ideal for urban areas. Each type offers unique advantages for efficiency and user experience.</p></p>
