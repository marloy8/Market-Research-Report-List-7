<p><p>The PVA water-soluble film market is categorized into two main types: cold water-soluble films and hot water-soluble films. Cold water-soluble films dissolve in low-temperature water, making them suitable for applications like laundry pods and packaging for sensitive products. Hot water-soluble films, on the other hand, require higher temperatures to dissolve, often used in industries such as agriculture for mulch films and in pharmaceuticals. Both types offer eco-friendly solutions by enabling easy disposal and reducing plastic waste.</p></p>
