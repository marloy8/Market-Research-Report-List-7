<p><p>The Point of Use (POU) water filters market includes various types such as Gravity POU Water Filters, UV POU Water Filters, and RO POU Water Filters. Gravity filters rely on gravitational force to purify water, making them simple and energy-efficient. UV filters use ultraviolet light to neutralize pathogens, providing chemical-free disinfection. Reverse Osmosis (RO) filters employ a membrane to remove impurities and contaminants, delivering clean water through a multi-stage process. Each type addresses specific water quality needs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
