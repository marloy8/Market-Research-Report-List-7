<p><p>ATM machines can be categorized into three main market types: Cash Dispenser, Automated Deposit Terminal, and Recycle Type. Cash Dispensers provide users with cash withdrawals, primarily serving as traditional ATMs. Automated Deposit Terminals allow users to deposit cash and checks without human assistance, enhancing convenience. Recycle Type ATMs combine both functions, enabling users to deposit cash, which can then be dispensed to other customers, optimizing cash flow and minimizing the need for frequent cash replenishment.</p></p>
