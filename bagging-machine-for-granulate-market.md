<p><p>Bagging machines for granulate markets are essential in packaging granular products, with two primary types: Vertical Form Fill Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Seal (H-FFS) machines. V-FFS machines create bags from a flat film and fill them while standing vertically, ideal for rapid packaging of small to medium volumes. H-FFS machines, on the other hand, operate horizontally, allowing for larger and heavier products. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in the packaging process for granulated materials.</p></p>
