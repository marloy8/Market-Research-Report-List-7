<p><p>The disposable centrifuge system market is segmented into filtration and sedimentation centrifuges. Filtration centrifuges utilize a porous medium to separate solids from liquids, ensuring efficient sample processing in environments like laboratories and biopharmaceuticals. Sedimentation centrifuges rely on gravity or centrifugal force to separate components based on density differences, often used for larger volumes in industrial applications. Both types offer convenience, reducing contamination risks and eliminating the need for cleaning, making them ideal for single-use scenarios in various sectors.</p></p>
