<p><p>Integral gear centrifugal compressors are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage compressors based on their design and operational capabilities. Single-stage compressors utilize one impeller to achieve a specific pressure increase, making them suitable for applications requiring lower pressures. In contrast, multi-stage compressors incorporate multiple impellers in series, allowing for greater pressure ratios and efficiency, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including petrochemical, gas processing, and refrigeration, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
