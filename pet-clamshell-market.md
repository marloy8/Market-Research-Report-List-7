<p><p>The PET clamshell market comprises three primary types: Mock clamshells, Two-piece clamshells, and Tri clamshells. Mock clamshells feature a hinged design that resembles traditional packaging but lacks a full seal, offering ease of opening. Two-piece clamshells consist of a front and back that fully enclose products, providing better protection. Tri clamshells add a third element, often serving as a unique design for improved visibility and accessibility. These variations cater to diverse product needs in retail and food packaging.</p></p>
