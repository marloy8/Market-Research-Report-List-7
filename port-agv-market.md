<p><strong>The Port AGV Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Transport AGV</li><li>Horizontal Transport AGV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Port Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) market is categorized into vertical transport AGVs and horizontal transport AGVs. Vertical transport AGVs are designed for lifting and moving containers or cargo between different levels within port facilities, enhancing efficient stacking and retrieval. In contrast, horizontal transport AGVs focus on the movement of goods across flat surfaces, such as transferring containers between cranes, storage areas, and loading docks. Both types optimize logistics operations, reduce labor costs, and improve safety in port environments.</p></p>
