<p><strong>The Iron Ore Pellets Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heat Exchange</li><li>Indirect Heat Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron ore pellets dryers are essential in processing iron ore for efficient handling and utilization. The market can be segmented into direct heat exchange and indirect heat exchange types. </p><p>Direct heat exchange dryers transfer hot gases directly to the pellets, providing rapid drying but may impact pellet quality. In contrast, indirect heat exchange dryers use a heat transfer medium, ensuring precise temperature control and minimal contamination, making them suitable for high-quality pellet production. Both types serve unique operational needs in the iron ore industry.</p></p>
