<p><ul><li>Plate and Frame Construction</li><li>Spiral Wound Construction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrodeionization (EDI) technology is primarily classified into two market types: plate and frame construction, and spiral wound construction. Plate and frame construction involves individual ion exchange cell modules arranged in parallel, allowing for easy maintenance and high efficiency in water treatment. In contrast, spiral wound construction features a compact, continuous design that maximizes surface area and enhances system performance while minimizing space requirements. Both types cater to various industrial applications, including water purification and semiconductor manufacturing, providing flexible solutions for deionization needs.</p></p>
