<p><p>The multifunctional microplate reader market includes various types designed for diverse applications. Filter type microplate readers utilize multiple optical filters to select specific wavelengths, allowing for precise measurements in assays like colorimetric and fluorescence tests. In contrast, raster microplate readers employ a scanning technique across the microplate, capturing data from multiple wells simultaneously. This method enhances throughput and versatility, being particularly advantageous in high-content screening and complex experiments, thus catering to evolving research and clinical needs.</p></p>
