<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Laminates</li><li>Multidirectional Laminates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber laminates market is primarily divided into two types: unidirectional and multidirectional laminates. Unidirectional laminates feature fibers aligned in a single direction, offering high strength and stiffness along that axis, making them ideal for applications requiring specific load handling. Multidirectional laminates, on the other hand, consist of fibers oriented in multiple directions, providing improved impact resistance and structural integrity in diverse loading conditions. Both types cater to various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment.</p></p>
