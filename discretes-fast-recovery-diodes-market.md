<p><p>Discrete Fast Recovery Diodes are semiconductor devices used for rectifying applications, known for their rapid recovery times. The market is segmented into two main types: Single Common Anode and Dual Common Anode. Single Common Anode diodes feature one anode shared among multiple cathodes, ideal for space-constrained designs. Conversely, Dual Common Anode diodes provide two anodes sharing a common cathode, enhancing circuit efficiency and handling higher currents. Each type caters to specific application needs, balancing performance and design flexibility.</p></p>
