<p><p>The Modular Cleanroom System market consists of two primary types: Mono-Block Systems and Modular Wall Systems. Mono-Block Systems are compact, self-contained cleanrooms that provide a controlled environment with integrated air filtration and temperature regulation, ideal for small applications. In contrast, Modular Wall Systems offer flexible configurations that allow for easy expansion and customization of cleanroom spaces, catering to larger operations. Both systems ensure compliance with stringent cleanliness standards while facilitating efficient construction and reconfiguration.</p></p>
