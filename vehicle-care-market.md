<p><p>The Vehicle Care Market can be categorized into two main types: Regular Maintenance and Irregular Maintenance. Regular Maintenance refers to scheduled services such as oil changes, tire rotations, and inspections aimed at keeping vehicles in optimal condition and preventing breakdowns. Irregular Maintenance encompasses unscheduled repairs and services typically prompted by unexpected vehicle issues or failures. While regular maintenance focuses on proactive care to extend vehicle life, irregular maintenance addresses problems as they arise, often leading to higher repair costs and potential safety concerns.</p></p>
