<p><p>Deep brain stimulation (DBS) devices for Parkinson's disease are classified into single-channel and dual-channel systems. Single-channel DBS devices target a specific brain region, delivering continuous electrical stimulation, which helps alleviate symptoms like tremors and rigidity. In contrast, dual-channel DBS devices allow stimulation of two different brain targets, offering a more versatile approach to treatment. This dual capability can provide improved symptom control and tailored therapy, accommodating individual patient needs more effectively in managing Parkinson’s disease.</p></p>
