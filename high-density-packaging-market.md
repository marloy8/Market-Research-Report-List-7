<p><strong>The High-Density Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The High-Density Packaging market encompasses various advanced packaging techniques, including Multi-Chip Module (MCM) packaging, which integrates multiple chips into a single module for improved performance and space efficiency. MCP (Multi-Chip Package) packaging involves integrating multiple die within a single package, enhancing functionality while reducing footprint. System-in-Package (SIP) technology combines various components into one package, optimizing design and minimizing size. Lastly, 3D Through-Silicon Via (TSV) packaging allows vertical stacking of chips, significantly increasing functionality and data transfer rates while saving space.</p></p>
