<p><p>The axial leads multilayer ceramic capacitors market comprises various dielectric types, each serving distinct applications. NPO (COG) capacitors offer stable capacitance across temperature and voltage, ideal for precision circuits. X7R capacitors provide moderate temperature stability with higher capacitance values, suitable for decoupling and filtering. Y5V capacitors exhibit significant capacitance variation with temperature, making them less stable yet cost-effective for non-critical applications. Z5U capacitors have broader capacitance and voltage variations, often used in consumer electronics. Other types include specialty formulations for niche applications.</p></p>
