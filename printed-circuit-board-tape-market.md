<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) Tape Market serves three primary applications: e-coating, plating, and powder coating. In e-coating, tapes protect sensitive areas during the electrocoating process, ensuring uniform coverage. For plating, they provide masking for selective metal deposition, preventing unwanted coating. In powder coating, PCB tapes safeguard specific regions during the powder application and curing phases, ensuring clean finishes. Overall, these tapes enhance the quality and precision of coatings applied to printed circuit boards.</p></p>
