<p><p>The Automatic PET Blow Molder market consists of three primary types: Extrusion Blow Molders, which use a continuous extrusion method to create hollow plastic products; Injection Blow Molders, where pre-formed parisons are injected and then blown into shape; and Stretch Blow Molders, which stretch the PET material in both axial and radial directions for enhanced strength and clarity. Each type caters to different production needs, including varying shapes, sizes, and applications in sectors like food and beverage packaging.</p></p>
